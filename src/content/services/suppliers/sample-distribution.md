### Speed is the key to textile sales

In the garment industry, designers and merchandisers work on tight deadlines. If they need a specific 240gsm brushed fleece for a sample, and you have to ship it from China or Turkey, you've already lost the order. The designer will simply pick a fabric that is already available in the local market.

To win in Bangladesh, your samples need to be "around the corner," not across the ocean. We provide the local infrastructure to make your products the easiest choice for factory design teams.

### Your local sample hub

We maintain a organized, climate-controlled space for your seasonal collections. When a factory asks for a swatch, we don't just "mail it." We deliver it in person, often on the same day. This speed is a massive competitive advantage. It allows the factory to include your fabric in their "proto" sample for their end-buyer (the brand), which is the most critical step in securing a bulk order.

### Closing the feedback loop

Most suppliers ship samples and then never hear back. They don't know if the fabric was rejected because of the price, the hand-feel, or the color. When we deliver your samples, we follow up within 48 hours to get the real story. We find out exactly what the designer thought and what the target price is. This data allows you to adjust your offerings and win more business in the next round.
