### Don't let your leads go cold

The textile trade shows in Dhaka (like DTG or the Denim Expo) are massive. Thousands of people walk through the doors. You collect hundreds of business cards. But when you return home, you're faced with a mountain of emails and a 4-hour time difference. By the time you follow up, the factory owner has already moved on to the next project.

We turn "booth visitors" into "paying clients." Before the show even starts, we can reach out to our network and invite key factory owners to your booth at a specific time. During the show, our team is there to assist with language barriers and to ensure every visitor's specific needs are recorded.

### Local logistics, handled

Traveling to Dhaka with samples, brochures, and booth materials is stressful. We handle the "hidden" logistics. We can store your samples in our local warehouse before the show, coordinate the booth delivery, and even help with hotel and transport bookings for your team. We ensure that when you walk onto the show floor, everything is ready for business.

### The 30-Day Follow-Up

The real work starts when the show ends. In the first 30 days after an expo, we visit the most promising leads in person. We bring the samples they requested. We answer the questions they forgot to ask. This physical follow-up demonstrates your commitment to the market and is often the deciding factor in winning a new contract.
