### Post-sales support as a sales tool

In the Bangladesh textile industry, word travels fast. If a factory buys a knitting machine or a laser cutter and it sits idle for two weeks because they can't get a $50 sensor, they will never buy from that supplier again—and they'll tell their friends.

Reliable post-sales support is the single most important factor in building a long-term machinery business in Bangladesh. We provide the local coordination that makes your equipment the "safest" choice for factory owners.

### Managing the "Critical Path" of a breakdown

When a machine goes down, every hour is lost money for the factory. They need to know someone is listening. We act as your 24/7 communication hub. We take the initial report, gather the technical details (error codes, photos, videos), and relay them to your engineering team immediately.

We then coordinate the local response. If the part is in our local stock, we get it to the factory. If a technician is needed, we manage the schedule. By taking the "panic" out of the situation, we protect your brand's reputation for reliability.

### Spare parts and consumables

Don't let a small part kill a big relationship. We can manage a local inventory of your most frequently replaced parts. This eliminates the 5-day wait for an international courier and the complexity of customs for every small shipment. Your customers stay up and running, and you build the kind of loyalty that leads to repeat orders and upgrades.
