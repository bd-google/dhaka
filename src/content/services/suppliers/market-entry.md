### Finding the "Right" buyers in Bangladesh

There are over 4,000 garment factories in Bangladesh, but only a fraction of them are the right fit for your specific product. If you sell high-end Italian silk, you shouldn't be talking to a factory that makes basic t-shirts for discounters. If you sell specialized denim washing machinery, you need to find the specific laundry units.

The problem for foreign suppliers is that the Bangladesh market is opaque. Public directories are often outdated. We provide the "ground-level intelligence" you need to skip the cold calling and go straight to the factories that are currently buying what you sell.

### Direct access to decision-makers

In Bangladesh, business is hierarchical. Procurement managers can suggest a product, but the "Owner" or the "Managing Director" often makes the final decision on new suppliers or major machinery purchases. We have spent years building a network within the industry. We know who makes the decisions, and more importantly, they know us.

When we present your product, it isn't an "unsolicited email." It's a recommendation from a trusted local partner. This drastically increases the chances of your samples being tested and your quotes being reviewed.

### Competitive intelligence

To win a contract, you need to know what you're up against. We provide you with a "Market Reality Check." What are your competitors from China or India charging? What are the standard payment terms (L/C, TT, or deferred) for your product category? Armed with this information, you can tailor your proposal to be the most attractive option on the buyer's desk.
