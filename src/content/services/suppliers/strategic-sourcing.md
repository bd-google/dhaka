### Navigating the "Vertical" Bangladesh market

Bangladesh is no longer just a "cut and sew" destination. It now has a massive domestic industry for spinning yarn and weaving/knitting fabric. For a foreign supplier or a brand that is looking to increase their "Local Value Add" (often required for duty-free access to the EU or UK), sourcing materials within Bangladesh is a strategic necessity.

The challenge is that the mill sector is different from the garment sector. It's more capital-intensive, and the players are often large, vertically integrated groups. Finding the right mill for a specific yarn count or a specialized weave requires deep local knowledge.

### Independent mill vetting

Not every mill that claims to be "high quality" actually is. We conduct independent audits of the mills' facilities. We check their spinning frames, their looms, and their dyeing equipment. We also check their certifications. Does their "Organic" yarn actually come with a valid Transaction Certificate (TC)?

We act as your independent verifyer. We don't take commissions from the mills; we work for you. This ensures that the advice we give is based solely on your needs and the reality of the mill's performance.

### Managing local delivery and quality

Sourcing the material is only half the job. You also need to ensure it's delivered to your garment factory on time and in the right condition. We manage the "local supply chain." We check the yarn or fabric before it leaves the mill and coordinate the transport. By managing these local "moving parts," we ensure that your broader production goals are met without the typical "local sourcing" headaches.
