### Why ethics matter for small brands

In the past, only huge corporations like H&M or Zara had to worry about factory compliance. Today, even a small Instagram brand is expected to know where their clothes are made. "I didn't know" is no longer an acceptable answer for consumers or for customs authorities in many countries.

Bangladesh has undergone a massive safety transformation, but there is still a wide gap between "Tier 1" factories and the smaller units that often handle low-MOQ orders. We ensure that your production is happening in a place you can be proud of.

### Real-world compliance monitoring

We don't just look at the certificates on the wall. We walk the floor to see if the fire exits are blocked. We check the age of the workers. We look at the "punch cards" or attendance records to see if workers are being forced into excessive overtime.

If you have specific requirements—like wanting to ensure your garments are made in a woman-owned factory or one that uses solar power—we can find and verify those specific partners for you.

### Transparency you can share

When we finish our compliance check, we provide you with the data you need to be transparent with your customers. You can confidently state that your factory pays fair wages and provides a safe environment. In a world of "fast fashion" criticism, this ethical foundation is a powerful competitive advantage for your brand.
