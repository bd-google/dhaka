### The final hurdle: Logistics

You've navigated the factory search, the sampling, and the production. Your goods are packed and ready to go. But the journey isn't over. The final hurdle is getting the garments out of Bangladesh and through your home country's customs. This is where a single typo on a document can cost you thousands of dollars.

Documentation errors are the leading cause of delays at the port of Chittagong and at destination customs. If the weight on the packing list doesn't match the weight on the Bill of Lading, or if the "Country of Origin" is not correctly certified, your shipment can be flagged for inspection or held indefinitely.

### Document verification service

Before the factory sends the final set of documents to you or your bank, we review them. We check every line for accuracy. Does the quantity match what we saw during inspection? Is the description of the goods clear?

We pay special attention to **HS Codes**. Different garments (e.g., a 100% cotton t-shirt vs. a synthetic blend jacket) have different codes. Choosing the right one ensures you aren't overpaying on import duties while remaining fully compliant with your local laws.

### Coordinating the "Hand-off"

We also help coordinate the hand-off between the factory and your freight forwarder. We ensure the factory has the "Booking Note" and that the truck is scheduled to reach the CFS (Container Freight Station) before the cutoff date. By managing these small logistical details, we ensure your goods spend less time in a warehouse and more time on the way to your customers.
