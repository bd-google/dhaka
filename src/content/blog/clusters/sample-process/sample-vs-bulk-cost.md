### The Sample Premium

Buyers are often shocked to see a "Sample Invoice" for $150 for two hoodies. They think the factory is overcharging them. But a sample is a custom, hand-made garment.

A sample is made by a "Master Pattern Maker" and a senior "Sample Machinist" who are the highest-paid workers in the factory. They aren't using a production line; they are making the garment one-by-one.

Standard practice in Bangladesh is to charge **2x or 3x the estimated bulk price** plus the cost of express couriers. Most factories will offer to **refund or credit** these sample costs once you place a bulk order of a certain volume (usually 1,000+ pieces).
