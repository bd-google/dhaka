### The Last Mile of Machinery Sales

You've sold the machine, and it has arrived at the factory. Now comes the hard part: Installation.

In Bangladesh, factories often have limited floor space and specific power/water requirements. Before the machine arrives, a local coordinator should conduct a "Pre-Installation Audit." Are the power points correct? Is there enough clearance for the technicians?

Having a local team to manage the crane hire, the local electricians, and the "unboxing" ensures that when your expert engineer arrives from overseas, they can spend their time on **commissioning**, not on fixing basic site issues.
