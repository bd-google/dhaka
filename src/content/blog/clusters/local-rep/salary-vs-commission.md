### Incentives and Control

If you are a foreign supplier entering Bangladesh, how should you pay your local partner?

**Commission (Success-based)**:
* **Pros**: No upfront cost. The agent only gets paid when you get paid.
* **Cons**: The agent will prioritize your largest orders and may ignore the small "development" work that leads to future business. You have very little control over their daily schedule.

**Flat Fee / Monthly Retainer**:
* **Pros**: You are their priority. You can demand daily reports and specific factory visits. They act as your true "Liaison Office."
* **Cons**: A fixed monthly cost regardless of sales volume.

For companies serious about building a brand in Bangladesh, we recommend a **Hybrid Model**: a modest monthly retainer to cover their time and office costs, plus a smaller commission to reward sales performance.
