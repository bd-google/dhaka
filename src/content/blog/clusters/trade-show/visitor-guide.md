### Navigating the Expo

The Dhaka Apparel Expo is the heartbeat of the industry. If you are visiting for the first time:

1. **Stay in Uttara or Gulshan**: Most expos are at the ICCB. Staying in Uttara puts you within 20 minutes of the venue. Staying in downtown Dhaka (Motijheel) can mean a 3-hour commute in traffic.
2. **Business Cards are Gold**: Bring at least 500. You will hand them out like candy.
3. **The "Tea" Meeting**: If a factory owner invites you for tea at their booth, accept it. It is a sign of respect and the start of the "real" negotiation.
4. **Follow-up on WhatsApp**: In Bangladesh, WhatsApp is the primary business tool. After the meeting, send a quick "Nice to meet you" message to keep the connection alive.
