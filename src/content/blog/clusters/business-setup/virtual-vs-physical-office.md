### The "Address" Requirement

When you apply for a Liaison Office through BIDA, you must provide a physical address. Many foreign companies ask if a "Virtual Office" or a "Shared Coworking Space" is enough.

The short answer is **No**. BIDA inspectors will physically visit the office before granting final approval. They want to see a dedicated space with a company sign, furniture, and a telephone line. However, for the initial application, you can use a "Letter of Intent" from a landlord. Once approved, you have a set number of days to finalize the lease and move in.
