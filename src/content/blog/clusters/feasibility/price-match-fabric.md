### The 60% Rule

In a typical Bangladesh garment, the raw material (fabric and trims) accounts for roughly 60% of the total price. If you want a $3.00 t-shirt, the fabric must cost no more than $1.80.

If you specify a GOTS-certified organic cotton with a specialized "peached" finish, that fabric alone might cost $2.50 per garment. No amount of "negotiation" with the factory can bridge that gap.

Before you commit to a target price, ask a local sourcing expert for a "Material Reality Check." We can tell you the current market rate for yarn and dyeing, so you know exactly what is possible.
