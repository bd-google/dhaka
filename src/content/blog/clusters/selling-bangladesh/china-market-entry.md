### The China Dominance

Currently, a massive percentage of the non-cotton fabric used in Bangladesh (synthetics, technical blends, high-end knits) comes from China. Chinese suppliers have mastered the "Dhaka presence."

They don't just ship containers; they have local warehouses in Uttara and Banani. They have local Bengali-speaking staff who visit factories every day. If you are a supplier from India, Turkey, or Vietnam, this is your competition. To win, you must match their **accessibility** and their **local stock** levels.
